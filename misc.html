Transportation & Healthcare

Phoenix has commutes that are shorter than the average US commuter. People tend to drive by car since there’s not too much traffic in Phoenix. Thanks to Phoenix’s low cost of living and low tax rates, Phoenix is shown to be one of the best places to retire in. MorningStar at Arcadia has won the design for excellence award for senior living, The Haven of Phoenix is known for their speech pathology, physical and occupational therapy, and the Phoenix Mountain Nursing Center has received excellent ratings for seven years in a row.
